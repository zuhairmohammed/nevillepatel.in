Neville Patel

ABOUT ME

Working with entrepreneurs & leaders to solve problems

Entrepreneurship, business development & business networking.

Born and brought up in Bombay, now Mumbai, Neville has been working since he was in School. What started as vacation jobs in Vitta Mazda, a stock broking company, helped him understand how small profitable businesses are run & made him more interested in entrepreneurship, business development & business networking.


Neville now has over 34 years of work experience in varied industries like IT & ITeS, BPO & KPO, Gaming & Interactive Entertainment, Mobile Gaming & Casino Gaming, Advertising & Product Launches, Telecom, Banking & Insurance, Human Capital Management, Data Annotation Services & Analytics.


In 2014 Neville Patel founded Qualitas Global QA Services a Game Testing & Game Development studio. Qualitas Global QA works with multiple Game Development Studios over Europe & North America. They have developed Mobile Games like Ludo, Diddle & lot more for multiple major customers. Qualitas Global is a premium Game Testing Services firm, having tested over 170 Mobile Games.


In 2015 Neville Patel founded Qualitas Global Services, one of India’s first few Data Annotation Services company working with Trillion Dollars companies like Apple amongst dozens of others.


In 2017 Neville Patel founded Qualitas Global Services B.V. in The Netherlands. The company now has a registered office in Utrecht and is a member of the Dutch Games Association.


Today, Neville Patel is considered an expert in the Data Annotation Services industry & is credited in setting up one of the first Data Annotation Services companies in India, along with maybe the only few in the world that provides Human-In-The-Loop Annotation services to Computer Vision, Machine Learning & major Tech companies for all industries ranging from Autonomous Vehicles, Retail, Drones, Security & Surveillance, Facial Recognition, License Plate Recognition & Vehicle Monitoring, Medical-Tech, Health-Tech, Agri-Tech, Underwater Annotations, LiDAR & RADAR, Airport Baggage & Airport Security to name a few.


Neville Patel wants to create a vision that inspires other entreprenuers & spend his time working with entrepreneurs, startup founders & leaders to solve business problems.


Neville Patel is also an Angel Investor & a business turnaround specialist. His speciality is investing his time & energy into loss making companies & turning them profitable.


He is currently looking for borderline loss making & low profit making companies to mentor & invest in, especially from Pune.
